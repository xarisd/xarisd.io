About me

Hi (again)! I am Theocharis (Haris) Dimitriou, born, raised and living in the beautiful Thessaloniki, in Greece.

I am a software engineer with over 14 years of experience in software systems implementations, having built various commercial enterprise systems for health, S.M.E.s, education, industry and culture.

I have a degree in Applied Informatics from University of Macedonia. I worked as a Delphi, Oracle Forms, Java and .Net developer before seeing the light and becoming a Ruby developer :-)

I am currently a co-founder and CTO at Polyptychon, an agency specializing in providing technology, content and exhibition services to culture and tourism organizations.

When I'm not writing code or taking care of my family, I will be probably trying to work wood or speaking about (not only) Ruby in the local meetups.

You can contact me via Twitter or email, you can find my full cv at LinkedIn and view my code/projects on Github.
